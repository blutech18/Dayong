# DAYONG Member Assistance & Collection Management System

## Project Features for Client Confirmation

## 1. User Roles

The system will have role-based access to ensure proper security and accountability.

### Administrator

The Administrator will have full access to all modules, including:

- Dashboard
- Staff Management
- Member Management
- Assistance Management
- Collection & Contribution Management
- Financial Records
- Announcements
- Reports
- Documents
- System Settings
- User Roles & Permissions
- Audit Logs
- Backup & Restore

### Staff

Staff members will be able to:

- Register Members
- Update Member Information
- Record Collections
- Process Assistance Requests
- Verify Documents
- Generate Reports
- Manage Announcements
- Update Their Profile

Permissions can be customized by the Administrator.

## 2. Secure Login & Authentication

The system will provide secure access for authorized users.
Features include:

- Login
- Logout
- Password Encryption
- Password Reset
- Account Status (Active / Inactive)
- Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)
- Session Management

## 3. Member Management

The system will maintain a complete database of members.
Features include:

- Member Registration
- Member Profiles
- Member Search
- Membership Status
- Member Contribution History
- Assistance History
- Member Documents
- Profile Updates
- Printable Member Profile

Optional:

- QR Code Member ID
- Digital Member ID

## 4. Member Contribution Management

The system will record all member contributions.
Features include:

- Contribution Recording
- Contribution History
- Outstanding Balance Monitoring
- Paid & Unpaid Status
- Collection Reports
- Printable Official Receipt

The system will automatically maintain a Contribution Ledger for every member.

## 5. Assistance Management

The organization can manage all assistance requests.
Assistance may include:

- Hospitalization Assistance
- Bereavement Assistance
- Dental Assistance
- Senior Citizen Assistance
- Medical Assistance
- Emergency Assistance
- Other Assistance Programs

Each request will include:

- Applicant Information
- Assistance Type
- Required Documents
- Approval Status
- Release Date
- Amount Approved
- Assistance History

## 6. Assistance Approval Workflow

The assistance process will follow a structured workflow.

1. Member submits request.
2. Staff verifies requirements.
3. Administrator reviews application.
4. Administrator approves or rejects request.
5. Payment is recorded.
6. Assistance funds are released.
7. Transaction is archived.

This workflow improves transparency and accountability.

## 7. Collection Events

The system will manage scheduled collection activities.
Features include:

- Collection Schedule
- Assigned Collector
- Collection Status
- Payment Recording
- Collection Reports

## 8. Financial Management

The system will record all financial transactions.
Features include:

- Income Records
- Assistance Expenses
- Collection Records
- Cash Flow Summary
- Financial Ledger
- Transaction History
- Printable Financial Reports

## 9. Official Receipts

The system will generate printable receipts.
Features include:

- Receipt Number
- Member Information
- Payment Details
- Date
- Amount
- Printable PDF Receipt

## 10. Document Management

The system will securely store important files.
Examples include:

- Member Requirements
- Assistance Documents
- Certificates
- Financial Documents
- Organization Files

Administrators can:

- Upload
- Replace
- Archive
- Download
- Delete Documents

## 11. Announcements

The organization can post important announcements.
Examples include:

- Upcoming Collections
- Meetings
- Activities
- Assistance Programs
- Public Notices

Members can view announcements from the system.

## 12. Dashboard

The dashboard will provide an overview of organizational activities.
It may display:

- Total Members
- Active Members
- Total Collections
- Total Assistance Released
- Pending Requests
- Approved Requests
- Monthly Collections
- Monthly Expenses
- Financial Summary
- Recent Activities
- Charts & Statistics

## 13. Reports

The system will generate comprehensive reports.
Reports include:

- Member Reports
- Collection Reports
- Contribution Ledger
- Assistance Reports
- Financial Reports
- Cash Flow Reports
- Monthly Reports
- Annual Reports

Reports can be:

- Printed
- Downloaded as PDF
- Exported to Excel

## 14. Notifications & Reminders

The system can automatically notify staff and members.
Examples include:

- Upcoming Collection Schedule
- Outstanding Contributions
- Approved Assistance
- Rejected Assistance
- Document Requirements
- Announcement Notifications

Notifications may be sent through email.

## 15. Audit Logs

For accountability, the system will record user activities.
Examples include:

- Login History
- Record Creation
- Record Updates
- Record Deletion
- Assistance Approval
- Payment Recording
- Document Uploads

## 16. Backup & Restore

The Administrator can protect organizational data through:

- Manual Database Backup
- Restore Database
- Download Backup Files

## 17. Overall Workflow

1. Administrator creates staff accounts.
2. Staff registers members.
3. Member contributions are recorded.
4. Collection activities are monitored.
5. Members submit assistance requests.
6. Staff verifies documents.
7. Administrator approves or rejects requests.
8. Approved assistance is released and recorded.
9. Financial reports are automatically updated.
10. Members and staff receive notifications.
11. Reports are generated whenever needed.

## Goal of the System

The purpose of this web-based Member Assistance & Collection Management System is to centralize member records, contribution tracking, financial management, assistance processing, document management, and reporting into one secure and easy-to-use platform.

The system aims to improve operational efficiency, financial transparency, accountability, and record management while providing faster and more organized services for members.
